# MyBodyCam Privacy Policy

MyBodyCam respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

## Information Collected
MyBodyCam may collect video, audio, location data, and account information solely for the purpose of providing user-initiated personal safety sessions.

## Use of Information
Collected data is used only to operate MyBodyCam features and preserve user-requested evidence. Data is not sold or used for marketing purposes.

## SMS Communications
Users may opt in to receive non-marketing SMS notifications. SMS consent is optional and may be revoked at any time by replying STOP.

## Data Security
Reasonable technical and administrative safeguards are used to protect stored data.

## Contact
Questions regarding privacy may be directed to MyBodyCam support.
