# MyBodyCam SMS Consent Disclosure

Users may choose to receive non-marketing SMS notifications from MyBodyCam related to personal safety sessions within the MyBodyCam mobile application.

SMS notifications are optional and are sent only after a user affirmatively opts in during account setup or feature activation.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

Users may opt out at any time by replying **STOP**. For help, reply **HELP**.

MyBodyCam SMS notifications are informational only and do not provide access to recorded content, do not control safety sessions, and do not replace emergency services.
