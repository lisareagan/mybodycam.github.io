# MyBodyCam SMS Opt-In Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in, you expressly consent to receive **SMS (text message) notifications** from MyBodyCam.

These messages may include:
- Safety session notifications
- Status alerts related to a MyBodyCam safety session
- Account-related notifications requested by you

Message frequency varies based on user activity.

Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP**.
For help, reply **HELP**.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not required to use MyBodyCam and is separate from acceptance of the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
